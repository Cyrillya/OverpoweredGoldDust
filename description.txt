This mod adds a new item - Magical Gold Dust
It has the ability to turn stones, critters, slime, chests, platforms, wood, walls, crates and coins into gold!
There is a crafting recipe to craft magical gold dust:
[Fallen Star x5] + [Gold/Platinum Bar x5] -> [Magic Gold Dust x3]
You should craft it next to a furnace.

此Mod增加了一个新物品 - 魔法金尘.
它有能力将石头、小动物、史莱姆、箱子、平台、木头、墙壁、宝匣、铜币和银币变成金子！
魔法金尘有一个合成配方:
[坠星x5] + [金锭/铂金锭x5] -> [魔法金尘x5]
需要靠近熔炉制作.
